Dollar-Cost-Averaging

...works to lessen the risk of investing a large amount in a single investment at the wrong time. It involves continuous investing regardless of fluctuating prices levels. Automatically, more share are purchased when prices are low and fewer shares are bought when prices are high. Dollar-Cost-Averaging does not guarantee a profit or assume a loss, investments are not FDIC insured, and you should consider your financial ability to continue purchases in a declining market.
